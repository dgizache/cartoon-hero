About Patrick

Patrick is a star. He hopes to one day get his degree in woodshop engineering so that he can pursue his dream job of being a cashier at the Krusty Krab.
His hobbies include eating glue, taking the wrong order at the front desk, and drawing yellow squares as his friends.
